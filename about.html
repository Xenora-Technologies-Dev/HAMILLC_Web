Company Overview

AL HAMIDIYAH GEN TR LLC (HAMI) is incorporated under Sharjah Economic Department, United Arab Emirates.

We specialize in providing comprehensive project sourcing solutions for technical and engineering items across diverse sectors including Marine, Offshore, Land, and Hospitality industries. Our strategic location in Sharjah positions us as a gateway to global markets, enabling efficient sourcing and distribution of quality products worldwide.

With years of experience in the industry, HAMI has established itself as a reliable partner for businesses seeking quality equipment, spare parts, and technical supplies. Our team of experts works closely with clients to understand their specific requirements and deliver tailored solutions that meet international standards.
