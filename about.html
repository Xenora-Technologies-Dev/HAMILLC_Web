Company Overview

Al Hamidiya General Trading LLC (HAMI — Hotel And Marine Innovations) is incorporated under Sharjah Economic Department, United Arab Emirates.

We specialize in providing comprehensive project sourcing solutions for technical and engineering items across diverse sectors including Marine, Offshore, Land, and Hospitality industries. Our strategic location in Sharjah positions us as a gateway to global markets, enabling efficient sourcing and distribution of quality products worldwide.

With over 15 years of experience in the industry, HAMI has established itself as a reliable partner for businesses seeking quality equipment, spare parts, and technical supplies. Our team of experts works closely with clients to understand their specific requirements and deliver tailored solutions that meet international standards.

As a UAE-based enterprise operating under the vision of the nation's leadership, HAMI aligns with the UAE's economic diversification strategy, contributing to the growth of the maritime, hospitality, and industrial sectors across the GCC and beyond.

What HAMI Stands For

More than a name — it's a mission that defines everything we do.

H — Hotel

Complete hospitality solutions — from commercial kitchen equipment and pool systems to guest amenities and HVAC for luxury hotels and resorts.

A — And

The bridge connecting our dual expertise — seamlessly serving both hospitality and maritime industries with a unified procurement approach.

M — Marine

Comprehensive marine supplies — engines, navigation systems, safety gear, deck equipment, and spare parts for vessels and offshore platforms.

I — Innovations

Innovative sourcing solutions — leveraging technology, global networks, and deep industry knowledge to deliver beyond expectations.

Rooted in the UAE

Proudly contributing to the UAE's vision of economic excellence and global trade leadership.

From our headquarters in the Emirate of Sharjah, HAMI operates at the crossroads of global trade. The UAE's world-class infrastructure — including Jebel Ali Port, Sharjah's industrial zones, and the nation's advanced logistics network — empowers us to source, store, and ship products with unmatched efficiency.

Our business model aligns with the UAE's focus on diversification, innovation, and sustainability. We contribute to the nation's thriving maritime cluster, support the hospitality sector that serves millions of tourists annually, and uphold the UAE's reputation for quality and reliability in global commerce.

HAMI is committed to the principles of UAE Vision 2031, fostering knowledge-driven growth and building sustainable partnerships across the supply chain.
